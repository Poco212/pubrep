# Phase 1: Pre-Production (The Blueprint)

- Define Goal & Audience: Who is this for? What is the goal?
- Develop Concept: Brainstorm ideas that fit VEAT, research competitors.
- Script & Storyboard: Map out scenes, dialogues, and visual cues.
- Logistics: Location scouting, gear check, talent acquisition.

# Phase 2: Production (The Shoot)

- Lighting: Ensure subjects are well-lit, use natural light if possible.
- Stable Footage: Use tripods or gimbals.
- Action: Capture main shots + B-roll.

# Phase 3: Post-Production (The Polish)

- Editing: Cut, trim, add transitions, and color correct.
- Add Assets: Incorporate graphics, text overlays, and audio.
- Feedback: Review and refine.
